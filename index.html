About Me

Hello and welcome potential employers! My name is Bill Kamberos and I want to start by thanking you for visiting my portfolio web page. I'm very excited for you to explore and experience all of projects and knowledge I have to offer. Before you dive in though, please take a minute to read about what lead me here.


My story begins as a young, nerdy, twelve year old. My mother needed a computer to write papers and complete assignments for her college degree. After purchasing one, I soon discovered that not only can this computer can do office type work, but it can also be used for media and gaming. Flash forward a few years and I'm building my first PC with my friends and learning everything about current PC hardware.


After high school I wanted to turn my love of computers into a career. I realized quickly that I enjoyed more of a creative approach to technology instead of the hardware/industrial aspect. Which led me to signing up for a coding camp to really propel my career in the right direction. I chose UPenn's coding camp and never looked back. I am now confident in my abilities and ready to start a new career as a full stack developer!
